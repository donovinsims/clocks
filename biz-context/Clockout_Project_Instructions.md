# Clockout — Project Instructions

---

This project contains the complete brand and content infrastructure for Clockout, a local automation practice serving owner-operated local businesses in the Rockford, Roscoe, and Beloit corridor of Northern Illinois. The qualifier is not the industry or the headcount — it is the situation: the owner is still the bottleneck. The files in this project define exactly who the business serves, what it does, how it communicates, and why it's different from the agencies and SaaS platforms that have already failed this audience. Use them as the source of truth for every piece of content, every outreach message, and every sales asset created here.

When generating content, treat the Brand Voice & Bio and ICP documents as a unified brief. The voice is peer-to-peer, math-first, and ruthlessly specific — short sentences, exact dollar figures, zero jargon, and no inspirational filler. The reader is an overworked local business owner scanning on their phone at 9 PM who has been burned by agencies before; every sentence must either expose a leak, prove the math, or explain the fix. When in doubt, lead with the problem and the dollar cost before any solution is introduced. The sale is always a logical conclusion, never a persuasion event.
